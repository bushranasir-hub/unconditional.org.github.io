About
Un-conditional Inc.

Our Story

More Than a Nonprofit — A Movement.

Un-conditional Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization established in 2015 with a clear mission: provide resources to pregnant and parenting teens to help them navigate the dual journey of youth and parenthood.

We believe every young parent already has the strength within them — they simply need the right tools, community, and opportunities to bring it forward. We meet teens where they are — without judgment or barriers — so they can shape their own stories with confidence.

"We shape our own stories. We don't let circumstances write them for us."

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2015
Established
Un-conditional Inc. was formed as a nonprofit organization to provide tangible support to pregnant and parenting teens in the metro-Atlanta area.
2016 – 2019
Events & Outreach
Signature events like "Unwrap Hope for the Holidays" brought our community together and amplified our impact across the metro-Atlanta area.
2020 – 2023
H.E.L.P. Initiative
We launched the H.E.L.P. Initiative — a structured framework built on four pillars to provide holistic support for pregnant and parenting teens across the metro-Atlanta area.
2024 – 2025
Enhanced Community Partnerships
We forged collaborative partnerships with organizations like Michael A. Grant Boys & Girls Club, Cobb Collaborative, WIC-Cobb Douglas Public Health, and others to extend our reach.
2026
H.E.L.P. Summit — December 2026
Our landmark H.E.L.P. Summit is scheduled for December 5, 2026 in Marietta, GA — bringing together teen parents, sponsors, volunteers, and community leaders for a day of empowerment.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Unconditional H.E.L.P.

We support every teen parent without judgment, without barriers, and without conditions. Everyone deserves to feel valued and seen.

Growth & Resilience

We believe all young parents have the capacity to grow, adapt, and thrive. Challenges are the soil in which resilience is grown.

It Takes A Village

We build a community where every hand helps and every heart supports. Together, we stand with young moms.

Empowerment

We equip young parents with tools, resources, and the confidence to face their unique challenges.

Access Without Barriers

All our resources are completely free. We work to remove systemic obstacles by focusing on outcomes that benefit both parents and children across generations.

Hope & Possibility

We believe the future is bright for every teen parent willing to write their own story. Together, we can build a future filled with possibility and success.
