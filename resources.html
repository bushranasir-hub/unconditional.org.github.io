H — Health

Your Body,
Your Wellness.

Physical and mental health resources designed specifically for pregnant teens and young parents. Because taking care of yourself is taking care of your baby.

H

Unplanned Pregnancy Support

Guidance, counseling pathways, and emotional support for teens navigating an unplanned pregnancy. You're not alone — we'll walk through this with you.

PregnancyCounseling

Mental Wellness

Resources for managing postpartum emotions, stress, anxiety and building a healthy mental state. Young parents experience real emotional challenges — let's address them head-on.

Mental HealthWellness

Nutrition Guidance

Healthy eating tips for pregnant and nursing mothers, access to WIC resources, and meal planning basics for your growing family — without breaking the budget.

NutritionWIC

Developmental Milestones

Track your child's development with confidence. Understanding what to expect — and when — gives young parents the edge they need to raise thriving children.

Child Dev.Milestones

Healthcare Navigation

Help finding prenatal care, pediatricians, and low-cost clinics in the Atlanta metro area. Navigating the healthcare system shouldn't be a barrier to your family's health.

Prenatal CareClinics

Family Planning

Comprehensive, judgment-free family planning resources and reproductive health education to help young parents make informed decisions for their future.

Reproductive HealthEducation

Your Future
Starts Here.

Education is the bridge between where you are and where you want to be. We remove the barriers that keep teen parents from reaching their academic goals.

E

High School Support

Resources to help teen parents stay enrolled and graduate on time. From tutoring connections to flexible scheduling options — your diploma is within reach.

High SchoolGraduation

Post-Secondary Pathways

College, vocational training, and certification programs that work for parents. Learn about financial aid, scholarships for teen parents, and flexible degree options.

CollegeScholarships

Dropout Prevention

Connecting at-risk teen parents with advocates, mentors, and school liaison programs that keep them engaged in education during one of the most challenging times of their lives.

MentorshipAdvocacy

Quality Child Care

Access to affordable and subsidized childcare options so teen parents can stay in school. Finding safe, nurturing care for your child should never hold back your education.

Child CareSubsidies

Online Learning Tools

Curated digital learning platforms, free courses, and study apps designed for busy parents who learn on their schedule, not someone else's.

E-LearningFlexible

Academic Advocacy

Know your rights as a student parent. We help you advocate for accommodations, extended deadlines, and school policies that recognize your dual responsibilities.

RightsAdvocacy

We partner with local schools, community colleges, and Boys & Girls Clubs to connect teen parents with academic support, tutoring, and scholarship opportunities.

Skills That
Last a Lifetime.

Real-world tools for navigating adulthood as a young parent — from managing money to building a career, we equip you with skills no classroom teaches.

L

Communication Skills

Workshops and resources on effective communication — with partners, co-parents, employers, and healthcare providers. How you communicate shapes your entire world.

CommunicationWorkshops

Time Management

Practical strategies for juggling school, parenting, work, and self-care. Young parents who master their time master their future — we'll show you how.

ProductivityPlanning

Problem Solving

Build critical thinking and decision-making skills. Learn frameworks for working through challenges — from childcare crises to difficult relationship decisions.

Critical ThinkingDecision Making

Financial Literacy

Budgeting, saving, credit basics, and financial planning for young families. The foundation of financial stability starts with knowing the rules of the money game.

BudgetingFinance

Workforce Development

Resume building, job interview prep, and career pathways for teen parents re-entering or entering the workforce for the first time. Your career story starts now.

CareerEmployment

Independent Living

Navigation resources for housing assistance, utilities, transportation, and the basics of running a household — the adulting skills everyone needs but few are explicitly taught.

HousingIndependence

Raising Kids
With Confidence.

Practical parenting guidance rooted in love, science, and community wisdom. Because being a great parent is a skill — and it can absolutely be learned.

Parenting Best Practices

Research-backed parenting techniques and approaches that help young parents build strong, healthy bonds with their children from day one.
