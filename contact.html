Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Who can receive support from Un-conditional Inc.?
Our resources are available to pregnant teens and parenting teens — regardless of age, race, background, or circumstance. We provide support without conditions or judgment.
Are all resources really free?
Yes — 100%. Every resource, program, workshop, and service we offer is provided at absolutely no cost to the families we serve. This is made possible through the generosity of our sponsors, donors, and volunteers.
How can I volunteer with Un-conditional Inc.?
We welcome volunteers (18 and older) who share our passion for empowering teen families! Simply fill out the contact form above, select "I'd like to volunteer," and tell us about your skills and availability. We'll reach out to match you with the right opportunity.
How can my company become a corporate sponsor?
We offer several sponsorship tiers with different levels of visibility and involvement. Visit our Sponsors page for more details, or contact us directly and we'll send you our sponsorship package with all the information you need to get started.
Is Un-conditional Inc. a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit?
Yes. Un-conditional Inc. is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization • EIN: 33-2817659, which means all donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent permitted by law. We have been operating since 2015.
What areas do you serve?
We partner with local organizations throughout Georgia to serve the greater Metro Atlanta area, including Fulton, Cobb, Douglas, Paulding, Dekalb, Clayton, and surrounding counties.

Ready to Make a Difference?

Join our community of supporters, volunteers, and sponsors. Together, we build brighter futures for teen parents — unconditionally.
