Who We Are

A Safe Space Built on Unconditional Support

Un-conditional Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit established in 2015 to provide resources to pregnant and parenting teens — helping them navigate the dual journey of youth and parenthood.

We believe every young parent deserves to shape their own story with confidence and resilience, surrounded by a supportive community.

"Dare to write your own narrative for a bright, promising future."

Our Framework

The H.E.L.P. Initiative

H

Health

Unplanned pregnancy prevention, physical & mental wellness, nutrition, and developmental milestones.

E

Education

High school & post-secondary support, dropout prevention, and access to quality child care.

L

Life Skills

Communication, time management, problem solving, financial literacy, and workforce development.

P

Parenting

Best practices, positive discipline, early child development, healthy relationships, and legitimation.
